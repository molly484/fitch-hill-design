A fully custom kitchen renovation in Paradise Valley that reimagined the entire floor plan, moving the kitchen to where the living room once stood and opening the space completely. White oak cabinetry with slab fronts, quartzite countertops, handmade tile, and a custom pantry with built-in spice racks and a styled bar area make this one of the most considered kitchens we have built.
