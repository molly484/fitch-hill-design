our belief

Fitch Hill Design creates interiors that balance beauty, function, and livability.

With a thoughtful and deeply personal approach, each home is designed to feel intuitive, refined, and connected to the people who live there. The focus is always on long-term enjoyment, not short-term trends.

about us

Fitch Hill Design is a boutique interior design studio led by Molly Loughney, creating thoughtful, cohesive homes through close collaboration with each client.

The work is rooted in the belief that a well-designed home should feel intuitive, personal, and supportive of daily life.

Because the studio takes on a limited number of projects, each client relationship is intentionally high-touch. Time, attention, and expertise are fully invested in every home, allowing for a design process that is both considered and deeply tailored.

When the fit is right, the result is not only a well-designed home, but a positive and lasting experience for everyone involved.

frequently asked questions

What is the typical timeline for an interior design project? ⌄

It depends on the scope. A single-room furniture project might take two to three months. A kitchen renovation design phase typically takes two to four months. Whole-home projects can take six months or longer.

How can I get an estimate for my project? ⌄

Start by submitting an inquiry through our contact page. We'll learn about your project and discuss budget during our first conversation. Every project is scoped individually.

When is the right time to reach out to Fitch Hill Design? ⌄

As early as possible. For new builds and renovations, the design should happen before construction begins. For furniture projects, reach out when you're ready to invest in a complete result.

What are the next steps to get started? ⌄

Submit an inquiry on our contact page. Molly reviews every inquiry personally and follows up within two business days. If the project sounds like a mutual fit, we'll schedule a conversation.
